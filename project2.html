An Evening of Coming Home

TAP TO SEE PROJECT

A nine-panel interactive digital comic in the mystery genre. A figure returns home to find it eerily empty — the story unfolds through exploration rather than a fixed sequence, with glowing cues guiding each interaction.

The narrative deliberately blurs the line between observer and inhabitant. By the end, whether the reader is watching the mystery or inhabiting it is left intentionally unsaid.
