Stuck in the Loop

3d figure here later

TAP TO SEE PROJECT

This assignment asked to create a website to present a short video we filmed in the beginning of the course. The short film we did is about a person who is moving in and out in a loop: even if he exits the hallway he’s walking in, he ends up there anyways. In the loop, there’re two persons whose actions get weirder and weirder all the time with each loop. At first they interact with the stuck person, but they get more dangerous for what was shown through the passive attacks. Apparently, even through the other exits the person can’t escape. Our video's aesthetic aimed to be somewhat eerie, mysterious, and open for interpretation.
For the website I chose to frame this film as a case file from the police or some investigation agency. I strived for a very minimalistic and “functional not fun” experience. I wanted the website to look more professional and scientific rather than to be fun with experience. Some simple interactions are implemented to not disturb the overall aesthetic I wanted to create.
