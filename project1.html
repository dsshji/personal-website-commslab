Stuck in the Loop

[ click to open project ]

A short film about a person trapped in a looping hallway — no matter which exit he takes, he always ends up back in the same place.

Two other figures grow increasingly erratic with each cycle, shifting from curious interaction to quiet menace. The aesthetic is eerie and deliberately open to interpretation.

The website frames the film as a case file from an investigation agency: minimal, functional, and clinical — with subtle interactions that don’t break the mood.
