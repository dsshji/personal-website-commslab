comms_lab_exam

3d figure here later

You have an exam in one week. Every few days, you're asked: what would you do?
This project is a small choose-your-adventure game with videos. The options range from studying and grinding past papers to going on a road trip, watching a pipe organ performance on an island, or pulling an all-nighter finishing a show. Whatever you pick, a first-person video plays showing you living that choice. At the end of the week, you swipe to reveal your grade.
The grading is intentionally a little unpredictable — if you pick the same option every time, the outcome is the opposite of what you'd expect. It's meant to feel less like a quiz and more like a story with a twist ending.
