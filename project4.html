comms_lab_exam

[ click to open project ]

You have an exam in one week. Every few days you're asked: what would you do?

A choose-your-own-adventure game with videos — options range from studying to road trips, pipe organ concerts on an island, or all-nighters finishing a show. Whatever you pick, a first-person video plays it out.

The grading is intentionally unpredictable: picking the same option every time gives the opposite outcome you'd expect. Less a quiz, more a story with a twist ending.
