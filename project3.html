The Homecoming

3d figure here later

This project aims to tell a story about a burnt-out office worker trying to find internal peace again by coming back to the countryside. This story is special because it is told primarily through sounds, which are embedded throughout the website.
Our concept is to display abstract elements of art (shapes, lines, etc.) that visualizes the atmosphere of the specific scene. Meanwhile, sounds tell the story and minimal text supports the narration. Since this story is told through a website, we also aim to utilize web features, particularly interaction. The story is told from the first person perspective, which enhances the users’ empathy as they are able to explore and be in the shoes of the main character.
